The Butterfly Lovers (梁祝) is a traditional Chinese legend and love story about the pair Liang Shanbo (梁山伯) and Zhu Yingtai (祝英台). Due to circumstance and fate, the two are unable to be together, leading to both of their tragic deaths. Their spirits turn into a pair of butterflies and depart their graves, finally together again. This interactive piece is a product of both our music and artistic interpretation of the legend. Alternate between mousedown and mouseup to see the full range of the piece.

Music created by Kaijie Chen. Frontend interactive created by Lucy Zhang. Concept by Kaijie & Lucy.
